Privacy and Cookie Policy

Michael Dors utilizes cookies to enhance your experience and improve the website's performance. Cookies are small files stored on your browser to save preferences, track usage, and gather analytical data. Here’s how we use them:




Types of Cookies We Use

We use two main types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are crucial for the basic functioning of parts of the website, such as saving your preferences in the Countdown options/settings menu.
  • Analytics Cookies: We use Google Analytics to gather information about how users interact with our site. This helps us understand visitor behavior and improve our services.



Use of Cookies

We utilize cookies to save your accessibility settings and other personal options in Countdown, which are only stored when enabled and deleted when disabled. Analytics cookies are used to track and report website traffic and usage statistics. These statistics are not personally identifiable and are utalized to make informed changes to our services.

Click here to learn about Google's use of cookies.


Cookie Duration

Countdown cookies are saved for 70 days or until the setting is disabled. Google Analytics cookies vary in duration depending on their purpose (session-based or persistent).




Managing Cookies

You can avoid optional cookies in several ways:

- Avoid using Countdown personal options to prevent cookies from being placed on your device.
- Use the Countdown "Reset All" option in the options menu to clear all personal options cookies, then reload the page.
- Manually reset/disable each preference through the personal section of the Countdown options menu.
- Remove cookies through your browser’s UI.




Consent and Transparency

We inform users about our use of cookies and seek consent before placing non-essential cookies on your device. By consenting and/or continuing to use our site, you agree to our use of cookies.
