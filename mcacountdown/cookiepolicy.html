Cookie Policy

This website utilizes cookies on the options/settings menu. Cookies are small files that store preferences, passwords, or other data in a browser. In our case, we use cookies to save your preferences in the options/settings menu.

We may use these cookies:
1. arorf - This cookie saves the “Autosync Clock” option, and has a true/false, or boolean, content.
2. coce - This cookie saves the “Disable Confetti” option, and has a true/false, or boolean, content.
3. atc - This cookie saves the “Disable Text Animation” option, and has a true/false, or boolean, content.

We use cookies to improve the overall user experience, and give them more accessibility options and general settings. We only save cookies when a preference inside of the options/settings menu is enabled.

You can opt-out of cookies multiple ways. If you simply do not use the settings menu, you will not have any cookies placed on your device. If you have already enabled options, you can click the “Reset All” option in the options/settings menu, or individually disable each preference.

If you have questions about cookies or anything else, feel free to reach out to us at contact@michaeldors.com
